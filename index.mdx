## Overview

Proletario Parásito Podcast Parser is a console application that retrieves the podcast's RSS feed, extracts structured metadata from each episode, and outputs a summary. It handles various title formats including numbered episodes, episodes with guests, and special entries, while also extracting Simpsons episode references embedded in descriptions.

<Note>
  This is an unofficial, fan-made project. The author is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official member of the Proletario y Parásito podcast or its creators.
</Note>

## Technology Stack

- **Language:** C# 12
- **Framework:** .NET 8
- **XML Parsing:** System.Xml.Linq (XDocument)
- **Pattern Matching:** System.Text.RegularExpressions (compiled Regex)
- **HTTP Client:** System.Net.Http.HttpClient

No external dependencies beyond the .NET 8 standard library.
